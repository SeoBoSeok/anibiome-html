Product Overview How To Use What's Inside

AnimalBiome™ Gut Restore Supplement For Dogs is a unique fecal microbiota transplant (FMT) in a capsule designed to restore dog health. Convenient capsules are an alternative to surgical FMT, which is equally effective but far more invasive. More powerful than probiotics and prescription diets, because seeding the gut with beneficial, dog-specific microbes helps to restore it to a more balanced state.


Addresses chronic digestive issues and skin conditions in dogs, including diarrhea, vomiting, constipation, itchy skin, and atopic dermatitis.


Helpful Supplement for Dog Digestive and Skin Issues:

  • Supports well-formed and easy to pass poop
  • Fast-acting and gentle
  • Restores digestive and immune health
  • Soothes the intestinal tract
  • Improves clinical signs in 80% of dogs
  • Promotes healthy skin and coat
  • Veterinarian recommended
